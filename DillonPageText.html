OHIO RULES THE GALAXY

Return to Twats

Dillonovich my boy, how we all miss you. It's much like how we miss when times were simpler when we were freshmen. I need a fine 'GOOD MORNING!' to snap me out of the CHEM 110 nightmare binge, right?

Dillon is our steady buoy. Nothing can faze this man, not even tuition and fees. When everyone is gloomy or having a rough time trying to get a grip on math, Dillon's mere presence would calm us down into some roaring laughter. Conversely, when everyone is ecstatic beyond wild abandon, who else would be down to Earth like Dillon?

Perhaps the secret to his serenity is in living a wholesome life. The man's a walking example of what we are all striving to achieve - be kind to our friends, steady on our course, genuine in our intentions.
